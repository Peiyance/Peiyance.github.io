Research Interest

We focus on trustworthy machine learning methods development for e-commerce and financial applications, including recommendation algorithms, time-series analysis, data mining, semi-/un-supervised learning, large language models and related applications such as recommender systems and financial quantitative trading.

My research experience has equipped me with an in-depth understanding of how artificial intelligence can be used to solve complex, data-centric problems. The idea of applying these methodologies to the financial field and contributing to quantitative area is profoundly motivating.

I'm looking for the quantitative research/trader internship in Hong Kong. Please feel free to drop me an email if your have a job position or other collaboration opportunity.
